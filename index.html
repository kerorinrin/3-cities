New York City

New York City is the most populous city in the United States. It is known for its iconic landmarks such as the Statue of Liberty, Empire State Building, and Central Park. It is also a global center for finance, media, and culture.

Paris

Paris is the capital and most populous city of France. It is known for its iconic landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, Notre-Dame Cathedral, and the Louvre Museum. It is also a global center for art, fashion, and cuisine.

Tokyo

Tokyo is the capital and most populous city of Japan. It is known for its modern architecture, technology, and fashion. It is also a global center for business and finance.
